# Casper Consensus

[TOC]


Casper consensus belongs to PoS. Casper's consensus is reached on blocks, not on chains like traditional PoS. It features a special penalty mechanism: if you submit a bet that Casper cannot process according to the contract, you will lose all your deposit. This is designed to prevent validators from submitting different bets in different worlds, thereby deterring malicious nodes from attacking the network. Offending nodes will not receive transaction fees and will forfeit their deposit, greatly increasing the risk for malicious actors.
